Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

Our Wash & Fold service includes professional washing with eco-friendly detergents, proper drying at optimal temperatures, careful folding, and packaging. We sort by color, check pockets, and treat visible stains at no extra charge. Your clothes are returned fresh, clean, and neatly folded.

Express Service provides 24-hour turnaround for urgent laundry needs. Schedule a morning pickup, and we'll have your items cleaned and delivered by the next day. Same-day service is available for orders placed before 10 AM. This service includes priority processing and expedited handling.

We dry clean all delicate fabrics including silk, wool, cashmere, linen, rayon, and specialty blends. Our experts handle suits, dresses, formal wear, leather, suede, and garments with special embellishments. Each item is inspected and cleaned using appropriate methods for its specific fabric type.

Subscription plans offer recurring service at discounted rates. Choose weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly pickups. Plans include priority scheduling, dedicated customer support, and up to 20% savings. You can pause, modify, or cancel anytime. Perfect for busy professionals and families with regular laundry needs.

We use professional-grade stain removal techniques tailored to each stain type and fabric. Our experts identify stains, apply appropriate pre-treatments, and use specialized cleaning methods. While we can't guarantee removal of all stains (especially old or set-in ones), our success rate is excellent. Stain treatment is included in all services.

Yes! Free pickup and delivery is included with all services. Schedule your preferred time through our booking system or WhatsApp. We serve 15+ neighborhoods with flexible time slots. Our drivers are professional, punctual, and handle your garments with care. Track your order in real-time from pickup to delivery.
